Zero poor in 2030

The Earth has enough resources for everybody to live dignified lives. However, man-made policies have createt unjust distribution of resources. As a result, 2.6 billion live in absolute poverty.

We ask all state leaders to commit to:

  1. Eradicating absolute poverty by 2030.
  2. Formulating and implementing poverty eradicating policies that are based on justice and human rights.
  3. Pushing for ambitious and binding Sustainable Development Goals.
